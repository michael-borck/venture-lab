Four tools for the founder's journey

🔥

Idea Forge

Generate innovative business ideas with AI-powered brainstorming. A creativity dial lets you range from safe and sensible to wonderfully weird.

🌍

Global Compass

Analyse market opportunities across regions and industries with AI-driven insights — market size, competition, cultural factors, and entry strategy.

🎤

Pitch Perfect

Rehearse your pitch and get coaching: scores, strengths, and concrete improvement suggestions before you face a real audience.

📋

PRD Generator

Turn an idea into a comprehensive Product Requirements Document through a guided, step-by-step AI workflow.

Bring your own AI

VentureLab talks to the provider you configure — one settings tab each, with a connection test built in.

Ollama

The default. Runs models locally on your machine — free, private, and fully offline.

OpenAI

GPT models via your own API key, with usage tracked so costs never surprise you.

Anthropic

Claude models for thoughtful long-form coaching and analysis.

Google Gemini

Gemini models with a generous free tier — a good classroom starting point.

Built for classrooms, safe by design

VentureLab was made for entrepreneurship education — which means it has to be cheap to run, easy to install, and careful with credentials.

  • Keys in the keychain. API keys live in your OS keychain, never in config files.
  • Usage tracking built in. See exactly what each tool and provider costs.
  • Customisable prompts. Every tool's prompt template can be edited to fit your course.
  • Open source. MIT-licensed, auditable, yours to fork.

Setup guides, provider comparisons, and troubleshooting live in the documentation.
